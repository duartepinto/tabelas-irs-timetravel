Methodology

This tool adjusts income brackets for inflation using Portuguese consumer price index data. Tax rates are calculated based on the progressive tax brackets for each year, and purchasing power is calculated relative to the selected base year.

Note: Inflation adjustments are approximate and based on annual averages.
